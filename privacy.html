Privacy Policy

Feed the Bunny · Effective June 23, 2026

Feed the Bunny is built to be private and quiet. The app collects no personal data, has no accounts, and sends nothing about you anywhere. Your game stays on your device.

Information we collect

None. Feed the Bunny does not collect, request, or transmit any personal information. We do not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, or any account sign-in to play.

Data stored on your device

The only data the app stores is your local game save, which is kept on your device by the system. This includes things like your carrot count, your growers and upgrades, and how far along your bunny has grown. This information stays on your device, is not sent to us or to anyone else, and is removed if you delete the app.

No accounts

There are no user accounts, logins, or profiles. You do not need to register to play, and there is nothing to sign in to.

No tracking or analytics

We do not track you. The app contains no third-party analytics, no advertising, no advertising identifiers, and no behavioral tracking of any kind.

No ads

Feed the Bunny shows no advertisements.

In-app purchase

Feed the Bunny is free to download and offers one optional one-time purchase called "The Full Descent." This purchase is handled entirely by Apple through the standard In-App Purchase system. Apple processes the transaction, and we never see or store your payment details. Apple's handling of that purchase is covered by Apple's own privacy policy.

Children

Because the app collects no personal data, nothing personal is gathered from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this policy and revise the effective date above. Since the app collects no personal data today, we expect any changes to be minimal.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out at mr.pocketwhale@gmail.com.
