Frequently asked questions

How do I play?

Tap to feed carrots to your bunny. As you go, you can buy growers that grow carrots for you automatically, and upgrades that make each feeding count for more. Keep tending to your bunny over the coming days and watch how it grows. It is meant to be played a little at a time, so feel free to set it down and come back later. It will be waiting.

How do I restore my purchase?

Feed the Bunny is free to download, with one optional one-time unlock called "The Full Descent." If you have already bought it (for example after reinstalling or switching to a new device), open the game, go to the unlock screen, and tap "Restore purchases." Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple Account you used for the original purchase. Apple handles the restore, so it should reappear at no extra charge.

I lost my progress. Can I get it back?

Your progress is saved locally on your device. Because the game has no accounts and keeps nothing on a server, progress can be lost if the app is deleted, the device is reset, or local data is cleared. We are not able to recover a save once it is gone. If your progress vanished but the app is still installed, please email us with the details and we will do our best to help you sort out what happened.
